PeerMentor

Project Proposal by Gerry Boylan

Description

PeerMentor is a website that will be designed to put University students seeking internships in contact with University students who have completed those internships. The students seeking the internships can then be mentored by someone who has gone through the application process successfully in recent years. When someone visits the website, they will be able to browse through potential mentors where they can view that mentors work experience, education and bio until they find a mentor that interests them.

Objective

The objective of this website is to offer students much needed assistance throughout the job/internship application process from people a year or 2 ahead of them at school who have gone through the whole process. I have encountered this problem before and the current solution of reaching out to strangers on LinkedIn and hoping they reply is not up to scratch.

Target Audience

My target audience consists of university students seeking professional work experience. Nowadays it is nearly a necessity to have professional work experience upon leaving university for business/tech/engineering majors so this is a sizeable target audience.

It will additionally have a Top Mentors sections where they can quickly chat to the best rated mentors on the site.
